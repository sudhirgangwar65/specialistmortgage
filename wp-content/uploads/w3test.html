The Mortgage Process.

Step 1: Initial consultation.

Our broker will ask you questions, explain the loan process and answer all your questions.

Step 2: Preliminary Credit Assessment.

Our brokers conduct a review your financial situation and objectives to determine loan suitability.

Step 3: Loan recommendations.

Our brokers present suitable loan options, explaining costs, benefits, and drawbacks.

Step 4: Mortgage application.

You submit documents and evidence to verify your identity, income, and financial position.

Step 5: Pre-Approval (Conditional).

The lender indicates that you’re eligible to apply for a home loan up to a certain limit.

Step 6: Property selection.

You’ve entered into a contract of sale to purchase a home either by private treaty or auction.

Step 7: Property valuation.

The lender uses a valuation to confirm the property value and determine the loan value ratio.

Step 8: Formal Approval (unconditional).

The lender has approved your mortgage to purchase the home.

Step 9: Settlement.

The property title is transferred into your name and your mortgage commences.

Step 10: Congrats, you’re a homeowner!

Time to enjoy your new home or investment property!
